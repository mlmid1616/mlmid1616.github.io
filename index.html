ABOUT ME

I am a web developer with experience using React.js, Javascript, and Ruby on Rails. A while back I realized I didn't like to do things I didn't like to do, so I started to code. Problem solved!

Throughout my time in publications, non-profits, and finance, group work is where I thrive. I really enjoy solo programming, but in my experience a hard-working team that respects, challenges, and co-operates with each other is fertile ground for the cleanest and most creative solutions.
